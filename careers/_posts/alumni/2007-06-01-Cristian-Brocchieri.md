Dr. Brocchieri finished his PhD in Oncology from the University of Cambridge in 2006 and was a Visiting Clinical Research Fellow at BC Cancer in 2007.  After that, he was part of [Alexion Pharmaceuricals](https://alexion.com/) for 9 years, from 2010 to 2016, starting as a Medical Advisor and becoming the Country Medical Director of Italy.  Since 2019 October, he has been the Global Medical Director for [Roche](https://www.roche.com/).
