After receiving his BSc in Biochemistry and Molecular Biology in 2012, Charles joined the Aparicio Lab in 2012 and graduated in 2019 with a PhD in Pathology and Laboratory Medicine for his work on the [Signatures of genomic instability and DNA repair defects asdeterminants for tumour clonal dynamics and sensitivity toG-quadruplex stabilizers](https://open.library.ubc.ca/cIRcle/collections/ubctheses/24/items/1.0378536).  Since graduating, Dr. Soong has been working as an Associate for [McKinsey and Company](https://www.mckinsey.com/) in Tokyo, Japan.
