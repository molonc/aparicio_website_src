Dr. Courteney Lai was co-supervised by Dr. R. Keith Humphries (Terry Fox Laboratory, Vancouver, BC) and Dr. Samuel A. Aparicio (Molecular Oncology, BC Cancer Agency Research Centre, Vancouver, BC), graduating in 2017 with a PhD in Experimental Medicine at UBC with her thesis on [Insights into leukemogenesis in a MN1-induced leukemia model](https://open.library.ubc.ca/cIRcle/collections/ubctheses/24/items/1.0343276).  She continued her career as a Postdoctoral Researcher at the Hannover Medical School in Hannover, Germany.  Since 2021, she returned to Vancouver, Canada and now works as a Research Scientist at AbCellera.
