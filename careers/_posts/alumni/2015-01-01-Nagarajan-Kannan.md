Dr. Kannan received his PhD in 2008 in Molecular Medicine at the University of Auckland, and then proceeded to do two Postdoctoral Fellowships at BC Cancer from 2009 to 2015 with Drs. Dr. Connie Eaves and Sam Aparicio.   Since 2016, Dr. Kannan has been an Associate Faculty Member of the [National Cancer Institute Mayo Clinic Cancer Centre](https://www.mayo.edu/research/faculty/kannan-nagarajan-ph-d-m-s/bio-20233074), where he leads the [Stem Cell and Cancer Biology Laboratory](https://www.mayo.edu/research/labs/stem-cell-and-cancer-biology/overview).
