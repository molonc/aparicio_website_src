Farhia received her MBBS in 2001 from the University of Punjab and did her a MSc degree in Experimental Medicine at McGill University.  After graduating she, joined the Aparicio Lab in 2015 and received her PhD in 2021 May for her work on the Dynamics of single cell genomes and transcriptomes in response to chemotherapies.
